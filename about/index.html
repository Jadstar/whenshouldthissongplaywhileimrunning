About Locomotive

Locomotive is designed to enhance your running experience by selecting the perfect song for every moment.

Using data from your heart rate, cadence, and speed, we ensure you stay motivated and on track.

Our Journey

On November 27th, 2024, Spotify implemented some changes that impacted how the Locomotive app worked. Despite this setback, we're proud of the journey we've had.

Although we can't retrieve song characteristics anymore for the best song recommendations, Locomotive still allows you to control your Spotify app directly from your Garmin watch.
